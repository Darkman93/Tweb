CROSS COUNTRY

The cross country (XC) is the mountain bike discipline closest to classic cycling. It envisages running country or forest roads, and in any case carriageways, and low or moderate technical difficulty trails where driving ability is second to physical strength. Generally, those who are approaching mountain biking pass through the cross country just for this feature, as well as the fact that mountain bikes of lesser value are not suitable for more difficult routes.
