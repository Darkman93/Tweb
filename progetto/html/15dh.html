DOWNHILL

The Downhill (DH) is a discipline that takes place completely downhill along 2 to 5 kilometers of slopes prepared on very steep slopes and with natural or artificial obstacles such as jumps, high graders, even more meters and sloping sections of rocks and roots. It is one of the disciplines that fall within the gravity field, relying on the gravity force for propulsion.
